# Interactive Envelope Surprise & Premium Redesign

I have enhanced the third page of your project with a beautiful interactive surprise and a modern, premium aesthetic.

## Key Features

### 1. The Surprise Envelope 💌
The long letter is now tucked away inside a beautifully designed envelope. 
- **Interactive Open**: Clicking the envelope triggers a realistic opening animation where the flap flips up and the letter elegantly "pops out" and expands.
- **Glassmorphism**: The letter paper uses a modern glassmorphism effect (frosted glass) with a soft blur and subtle borders.

### 2. Magical Background 🌌
The background has been transformed from a static gradient to a **dynamic mesh gradient**.
- It features a blend of deep midnight blue, romantic pinks, and elegant purples.
- **Floating Stars**: I added a subtle layer of floating sparkles that slowly drift upwards, giving the page a magical, dream-like atmosphere.

### 3. Refined Layout & Aesthetics ✨
- **Enhanced Video Frame**: The video now sits in a more prominent frame with a neon-like glow and glass effect.
- **Micro-animations**: Added a "Click to open" hint with a gentle bounce animation to invite interaction.
- **Mobile Responsive**: The layout remains stunning on smaller screens, with the envelope and letter adjusting perfectly.

## How to Test
1. Open `index.html`.
2. Navigate through the first two pages.
3. On the final page, click the **Envelope** to reveal your special letter!
